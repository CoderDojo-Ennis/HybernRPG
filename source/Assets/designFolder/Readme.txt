Sprite Pack #1 - Tap and Fly 1.0.5
------------------------------------------------------------------

	This package is free. It contains sprites and sounds for helping you make simple 2D Tap and Fly games in Unity.

	Feel free for commercial or non-commercial use, we would be so grateful if you could credit to us.

	Features:

		• Pixel-art textures for sprites and UIs.
		• Three graphic themes.
		• Sample layouts and screenshots.
		• Sample sounds.

		• Support all build player platforms.
		
	Compatible:

		• Unity 4.6.9 or higher.
		• Unity 5.3.2 or higher.
